Top Shelf Tech Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

Overview

This privacy policy applies to all Alexa skills, web applications, and other software published by Top Shelf Tech and DrPine Solutions.

Data Collection

Our applications do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data or personally identifiable information. No user data is retained after a session ends.

Account Linking

Our skills and applications do not require account linking and do not connect to any third-party services that collect personal information.

Audio & Voice Data

We do not record, store, or process any voice or audio data. All voice interaction is handled entirely by Amazon Alexa in accordance with Amazon's own privacy policy.

Third Party Services

Our applications may use Amazon Web Services (AWS) for hosting purposes only. No personal user data is passed to or stored in these services.

Children's Privacy

Our applications do not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this privacy policy at any time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact
